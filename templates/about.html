Project Overview
This AI-powered resume analyzer represents a cutting-edge mini project that harnesses advanced Natural Language Processing techniques to intelligently compare resumes with job descriptions and provide comprehensive, actionable feedback.

The system employs sophisticated machine learning algorithms including TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity analysis to deliver precise matching scores and valuable insights for job seekers in today’s competitive market.
