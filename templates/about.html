About This Project

The AI-Based Resume Analyzer is a web application designed to help job seekers compare their resumes against job descriptions. It identifies matching skills, missing skills, and provides tips for improvement using natural language processing (NLP) techniques such as TF-IDF and cosine similarity.

This project uses Python, Flask, NLTK, and machine learning concepts for text analysis. The goal is to assist users in tailoring their resumes effectively to increase their chances of landing interviews.

Developed by Your Team Name as part of a semester mini-project.
